Accessibility matters

Probably you don't think of the importance of accessibility until:

  • You can't reach your security alarm turn off website from your Iphone while the alarm was turned on mistakenly
  • Your friend with impaired vision are unable to read your newly built website due poor design
  • Your favourite movie is not accessible from your computer due slow network connection

Now you might understand why accessibility of a webpage has a great impact in order to be found and used in all possible cases.

A life withouth accessibility - no thanks
